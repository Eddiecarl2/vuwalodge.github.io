About Us

Vuwa Lodge is situated in the heart of Polokwane, providing a great stopover for business or leisure travellers. Vuwa Lodge is conveniently located in Flora Park suburb, Polokwane, just a waling distance to Savannah Shopping Mall, Flora Med Clinic and Body Life Gymnasium. We are also 8 Km to the airport, 10Km to Meropa Cassino and Entertainment, Polokwane Game Reserve and many more.
